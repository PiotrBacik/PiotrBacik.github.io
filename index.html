About me

I'm a first year DPhil student on the Oxford-Max-Planck Doctoral Program (joint with the MPI-SWS institute at Saarland University, Saarbrücken) under the supervision of Joël Ouaknine and James Worrell.

My current research interests lie mainly in linear dynamical systems, in particular the Skolem Problem and questions surrounding zeros of linear recurrence sequences.

Most of my work uses techniques in algebraic number theory, which mostly involves bashing linear recurrence sequences with a p-adic hammer until something interesting pops out.

Previously, I completed a Bachelor's and Master's in Mathematics at the University of Cambridge.

If I have kept this up to date, I am currently in Oxford :)
